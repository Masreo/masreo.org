Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Our commitment to your privacy

Masreo is committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit masreo.org, why we collect it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Who We Are

Data controller

The data controller for this website is:

Masreo
Hull, United Kingdom
Email: hello@masreo.org

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us at the email above.

What We Collect

Data we collect and why

We collect two categories of data when you use this website:

Contact form submissions
When you fill in our contact form, we collect your name, email address, business name (optional), service interest and your message. This data is submitted via Formspree and used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Website analytics
With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised data about how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time spent on the site, general location (country or region level), browser type and device type. This helps us understand what is working well and improve the site over time. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Cookies

Cookies we use

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. This site only uses cookies if you choose to accept analytics tracking. We do not use advertising cookies or any other tracking.

If you accept analytics, Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

  • _ga: distinguishes unique users, expires after 2 years
  • _ga_0J5EE4C1XW: maintains session state, expires after 2 years

If you decline analytics, no cookies are set by this site. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Third Parties

Third party services

We use two third party services to operate this website:

Formspree processes contact form submissions on our behalf. Your form data is stored on Formspree's servers and forwarded to our email. View their privacy policy at formspree.io.

Google Analytics provides website analytics when you consent to cookies. Data is processed by Google LLC. View their privacy policy at policies.google.com.

Data Retention

How long we keep your data

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as necessary to handle your enquiry and any subsequent business relationship. If you ask us to delete your data, we will do so within 30 days.

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default, after which it is automatically deleted by Google.

Your Rights

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to have inaccurate data corrected
  • The right to have your data deleted
  • The right to restrict how we process your data
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to withdraw consent for analytics at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@masreo.org. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are unsatisfied with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

Updates

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We recommend checking back periodically.
