Common questions

Anything else? Just ask.

How do you get my data each week?

However suits you — WhatsApp, email or a shared Google Sheet. On Standard and above we set up a Google Sheet so your numbers flow through automatically and you don't have to send anything at all.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Give us 30 days notice and that's it. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.

How long does setup take?

We typically have your first dashboard live within 3 to 5 working days of you sending us your details. From there, updates run on your chosen schedule without you needing to do anything.

Do I need to be technical to use this?

Not at all. We send you a single link — you bookmark it, put it on a screen, or share it with your team. There's nothing to install, no logins to manage, and no software to update. If you can open a webpage, you're good.

Can I see an example before I commit?

Yes — just get in touch and we'll send you a sample dashboard so you can see exactly what you'd be getting. We'd rather you know what to expect before signing up than be surprised after.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, at any time. Just let us know and we'll adjust from your next billing date. No penalties, no awkward conversations — we want you on the plan that actually fits your business.

What if my business type isn't listed?

The list is just the most common — we build for all kinds of businesses. Get in touch and tell us what you track, and we'll tell you exactly how we'd set it up for you. If we can't do it well, we'll say so.
